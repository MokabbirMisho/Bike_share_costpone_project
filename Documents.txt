**Scenario**
The marketing team will develop marketing plans that would persuade casual users to become yearly subscribers. But to do that, the marketing analyst team must have a deeper comprehension of the distinctions between annual members and casual riders.

Three questions will guide the future marketing campaign:

1.How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?

2.Why would casual riders buy Cyclistic annual memberships?

3.How can Cyclistic use digital media to influence casual riders to become members?

I have been asked to find the answer for How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?
